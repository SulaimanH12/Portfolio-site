About Me

My name is Sulaiman. I'm a self-taught UI developer. I believe that if the design doesn’t improve the user’s experience, it’s purely decorative and of little use. I specialise in UI design and Front-End development. I am proficient in wireframing, implemtation of designs and more. I’m also proficient using HTML5, CSS3/SASS and Javascript.


In my spare time, I play and watch football with friends. I also like to game and read when I get time. I also write articles and manage the twitter account for Grup14.com.
